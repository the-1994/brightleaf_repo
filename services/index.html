Work Faster. Get More Qualified Candidates.

BrightLeaf's genius comes from our platform Appsyt, the most comprehensive recruiting analytics platform on the market. We uncover critical data from your job boards and ATS so your team can stop guessing and start recruiting. Set up in minutes.

What do we do with better data?

Get the Ones That Got Away

Your career website may lose as much as 90% or more of your potential applicants. BrightLeaf can get them back for you. We track who visits your site and give your ads a second chance with the ones that got away.

Predict and Attract Your Next Hires

BrightLeaf transforms your ATS data into a data-driven roadmap to your next hires. We use past performance to predict who should see your ads today. Stop waiting for qualified people to find you. BrightLeaf advertises to qualified candidates before they even begin their job search.

Make Old Applicants New

Stop relying on slow searches and stale emails to connect with your Talent Network or inactive applicants in your ATS. Think beyond the inbox. BrightLeaf delivers ads to your Talent Network to the places they spend most of their time: social networks and digital media.

Job Search Marketing

Optimize, and automate your Job Board Strategy. BrightLeaf will reduce your Job Board spend while increasing the reach and efficiency of you job search marketing budget.

Applicant Engagement

BrightLeaf ensures that qualified applicants get interviewed while unqualified applicants get properly tracked and stored away for another day. Recruiters can focus on recruiting and never look at an unqualified applicant again.
